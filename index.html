Strategy & The Evergreen Proposal

Our strategy focuses on scaling conversions and enhancing site authority. A key proposal involves transforming time-sensitive prediction content into valuable, long-term "evergreen" assets.

Strategic Initiatives for H2

  • Increase betting content production to 3x/week.
  • Continue optimizing legacy evergreen content.
  • Revise internal linking to boost page authority.
  • Execute a targeted backlink strategy.
  • Create dynamic tool pages using AI.
  • Expand high-conversion pages (e.g., "best of" rankings).

Evergreen Content Transformation

  • Phase 1 (Pre-Game): Publish predictions, stats, and odds.
  • Phase 2 (Post-Game): Update the same URL with results, analysis, and post-game statistics.
  • Key Benefit: This process preserves URL value, improves UX, strengthens E-E-A-T, and turns thousands of pages into a valuable, interlinked historical archive.
